Toggle deep-fried mode (uses distorted audio versions).

1. Run: `"$MOBE_PLUGIN_ROOT/scripts/toggle-setting.sh" deepFriedMode`
2. Show the new status
3. Test the sound by running: `"$MOBE_PLUGIN_ROOT/scripts/play-sound.sh" 2beep`
